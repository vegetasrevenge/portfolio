Meet teacher-turned-techie Casey Edwards

Born and raised in the Pineywoods of East Texas, hard work was the cornerstone of his upbringing.
A love of music (he might have sang "My Heart Will Go On" a few times as a child) led him to play the trumpet in band.
Talent didn't come to him quickly, yet hard work and passion carried him to a rewarding career as a band director.
Many hats he wore, such as: brass instructor, orchestra teacher, color guard director, middle school director, and occasional therapist.

Now, he comes to you as an aspiring developer! He specializes in Java, but has experience with front-end design with HTML/CSS and Javascript.
What has not changed is his passion for great results through vision and that East Texas work ethic.

Why not hop on over to his Contact Page and reach out?
Let Casey help create data and design solutions for you!
